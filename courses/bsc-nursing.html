The Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc Nursing) at RML Group of Institutions is a two-year professional degree programme that prepares graduates for a compassionate and technically skilled career in healthcare. The curriculum covers clinical nursing practice, patient care, anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, and community health — ensuring students are hospital-ready upon graduation.

Programme Structure & Course Details

Eligibility

  • Graduation in any stream — Arts, Science, or Commerce from a recognized university.
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in graduation.
  • 45% marks for candidates belonging to SC/ST/OBC categories as per Government of Madhya Pradesh norms.
  • Candidates appearing in final year graduation examinations may also apply provisionally.

Programme Highlights

Qualified Teaching Faculty

Learn from experienced nursing educators and healthcare professionals with advanced clinical and academic qualifications.

Clinical Practice & Internship

Gain hands-on patient care experience through structured clinical postings at affiliated hospitals and healthcare centres.

Seminars and Workshops

Participate in healthcare seminars, medical workshops, and guest sessions conducted by practising doctors and nursing specialists.

On-Field Opportunities

Engage with community health programmes, rural health camps, and hospital observation visits for real-world clinical exposure.

Career Outcomes

Staff Nurse

Work in government or private hospitals, clinics, and nursing homes providing direct patient care and support.

Community Health Nurse

Serve in public health programmes, rural health centres, and NGOs promoting community wellness and preventive care.

Nursing Educator

Teach at nursing colleges and training institutes after gaining clinical experience and further academic qualifications.

Healthcare Administrator

Progress into hospital administration and management roles overseeing nursing departments and patient services.

FAQs

Yes, B.Sc Nursing is one of the most promising healthcare careers. With India's growing healthcare infrastructure and increasing demand for qualified nurses in hospitals, clinics, and international healthcare settings, B.Sc Nursing graduates enjoy excellent employment prospects both domestically and abroad.

B.Sc Nursing graduates typically earn between ₹18,000 to ₹45,000 per month in India depending on the hospital, location, and experience. Government hospital nurses earn competitive salaries with additional benefits. International opportunities in countries like the UK, Canada, and Gulf nations offer significantly higher packages.

Admission requires submitting an online application along with graduation marksheets, identity proof, and passport-size photographs. Eligible candidates are contacted for document verification and admission formalities. Visit our Admissions page or contact our office for complete guidance.

The Government of India has significantly increased recruitment of nurses under the National Health Mission (NHM) and AIIMS expansion projects. State governments regularly conduct nursing recruitment drives for government hospitals. The Indian Nursing Council (INC) also regulates and promotes nursing education ensuring standardised national recognition of qualifications.
