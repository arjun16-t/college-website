Admissions

Admission Process

The admission process at RML Group of Institutions is straightforward and transparent. Follow the steps below to secure your seat in your preferred programme.

01

Check Eligibility

Review the eligibility criteria for your preferred programme. Ensure you meet the minimum educational qualification and percentage requirements before applying.

02

Submit Online Application

Fill and submit the online application form available on our website. Provide accurate personal, academic, and contact details. Upload required documents as specified.

03

Document Verification

Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for document verification. Bring original documents along with self-attested photocopies to the admissions office.

04

Admission Confirmation

Upon successful verification, complete the admission formalities and pay the prescribed fee to confirm your seat. Seats are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.

05

Orientation & Commencement

Attend the mandatory orientation programme before the academic session begins. Class schedules, faculty introductions, and campus orientation will be provided on this day.

Required Documents

  • 10th Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • 12th Mark Sheet & Certificate (for BNYS)
  • Graduation Mark Sheets (all semesters)
  • Graduation Degree / Provisional Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from last institution
  • Character Certificate
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card / Government ID Proof
  • Passport Size Photographs (6 copies)
  • Migration Certificate (if from other university)
