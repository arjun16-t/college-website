The Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharma) at RML Group of Institutions is a one-year professional diploma programme that equips students with foundational pharmaceutical knowledge and practical skills. The curriculum covers pharmaceutical chemistry, pharmacology, drug dispensing, and hospital pharmacy management — preparing graduates for immediate employment in the pharmaceutical industry.

Programme Structure & Course Details

Eligibility

  • Graduation in any stream — Arts, Science, or Commerce from a recognized university.
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in graduation.
  • 45% marks for candidates belonging to SC/ST/OBC categories as per Government of Madhya Pradesh norms.
  • Candidates appearing in final year graduation examinations may also apply provisionally.

Programme Highlights

Qualified Teaching Faculty

Learn from experienced pharmacists and pharmaceutical science educators with industry and academic expertise.

Pharmacy Lab Practice & Internship

Gain practical experience in drug dispensing, compounding, and pharmacy operations through structured lab sessions and industry internships.

Seminars and Workshops

Attend industry seminars on pharmaceutical regulations, drug safety, and emerging trends in the pharmacy sector.

On-Field Opportunities

Visit pharmaceutical companies, hospital pharmacies, and drug stores for real-world industry exposure and networking.

Career Outcomes

Pharmacist

Work at retail pharmacies, drug stores, and medical shops dispensing medications and advising customers on drug usage.

Hospital Pharmacist

Join hospital pharmacy departments managing drug inventory, patient medication records, and clinical pharmacy support.

Pharmaceutical Sales Representative

Represent pharmaceutical companies promoting and selling drug products to healthcare professionals and institutions.

Drug Inspector (Govt.)

Qualify for government drug inspector roles overseeing pharmaceutical quality control and regulatory compliance.

FAQs

Yes, D.Pharma is a highly practical and career-oriented programme. India's rapidly growing pharmaceutical industry and the expansion of retail pharmacy chains ensure consistent demand for qualified pharmacists. D.Pharma graduates can also pursue B.Pharma for further career advancement.

D.Pharma graduates typically earn between ₹15,000 to ₹35,000 per month in entry-level roles. Government pharmacist positions offer competitive pay scales with job security. With experience and additional qualifications, salaries can grow significantly especially in hospital pharmacy and pharmaceutical sales.

Candidates must submit an online application with graduation documents and identity proof. After document verification, selected candidates complete admission formalities and fee payment. Contact our admissions office or visit the Admissions page for step-by-step guidance.

The Government of India's Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) has created thousands of opportunities for registered pharmacists. State pharmacy councils conduct recruitment for government hospital pharmacist posts. The Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) regulates and nationally recognizes D.Pharma qualifications.
