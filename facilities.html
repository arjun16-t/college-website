Our Facilities

Campus Life

Built for Learning, Designed for Growth

RML Group of Institutions provides a comprehensive range of modern facilities to support academic excellence, student well-being, and holistic development.

01

Library

Our well-stocked library houses thousands of volumes covering education, nursing, pharmacy, naturopathy, and general knowledge. Students have access to reference books, journals, periodicals, and digital resources in a quiet, comfortable reading environment.

02

Auditorium

Our spacious auditorium serves as the venue for academic convocations, cultural events, seminars, and guest lectures. Equipped with modern audio-visual systems and comfortable seating, it creates an ideal setting for large-scale institutional events.

03

Hostel

Separate and secure hostel facilities are available for male and female students. Each hostel is equipped with furnished rooms, clean washrooms, a common room, and round-the-clock security — providing a safe and comfortable home away from home.

04

Canteen

The campus canteen serves fresh, hygienic, and nutritious meals throughout the day at affordable prices. With a varied menu catering to diverse tastes, it serves as a popular gathering spot for students to relax and recharge between classes.

05

Fundamentals of Nursing Lab

Equipped with anatomical models, patient simulators, and clinical equipment, our Fundamentals of Nursing Lab provides students with hands-on training in basic nursing procedures — preparing them for real hospital environments before clinical postings.

06

Community Health Nursing Lab

The Community Health Nursing Lab simulates real-world community health settings, training students in public health nursing, maternal and child health, immunisation, and disease prevention — essential skills for community-level healthcare practice.

07

Nutrition Lab

Our Nutrition Lab is equipped with modern kitchen infrastructure and dietary analysis tools, enabling students to learn therapeutic diet planning, food preparation for patients, and nutritional assessment — a core component of BNYS and nursing programmes.

08

Audio-Visual Lab

The Audio-Visual Lab enhances learning through multimedia presentations, medical videos, educational films, and interactive digital content. It supports faculty in delivering engaging lectures and helps students grasp complex concepts more effectively.

09

Computer Lab

Our Computer Lab is equipped with modern systems, high-speed internet connectivity, and essential software tools. Students use the facility for research, digital assignments, online learning resources, and developing basic computer proficiency essential in today's professional world.

10

Transport

RML Group of Institutions operates a fleet of buses covering major routes across Bhopal and surrounding areas. Safe, reliable, and punctual transportation is provided to day scholars, ensuring students can commute comfortably and arrive on time every day.
