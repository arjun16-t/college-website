The Bachelor of Naturopathy & Yogic Sciences (BNYS) at RML Group of Institutions is a five-year undergraduate degree programme that integrates the ancient wisdom of naturopathy and yoga with modern medical science. Students learn holistic healing techniques including hydrotherapy, diet therapy, acupuncture, yoga therapy, and physiotherapy — preparing them to become complete wellness practitioners and naturopathic physicians.

Programme Structure & Course Details

Eligibility

  • Class 12 (10+2) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) from a recognized board.
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in PCB subjects.
  • 45% marks for candidates belonging to SC/ST/OBC categories as per Government of Madhya Pradesh norms.
  • Candidates appearing in Class 12 final examinations may also apply provisionally.

Programme Highlights

Qualified Teaching Faculty

Learn from experienced naturopathic physicians, yoga experts, and medical science educators with extensive clinical and academic backgrounds.

Clinical Practice & Internship

Complete structured clinical rotations at naturopathic clinics and wellness centres gaining real patient care experience.

Seminars and Workshops

Engage in workshops on yoga therapy, diet counselling, hydrotherapy techniques, and holistic wellness management.

On-Field Opportunities

Participate in community wellness camps, yoga sessions, and naturopathy awareness programmes for practical exposure.

Career Outcomes

Naturopathic Physician

Practice as a registered naturopathic doctor at clinics, wellness centres, and hospitals offering holistic treatment.

Yoga Therapist

Work as a certified yoga therapist at rehabilitation centres, hospitals, corporate wellness programmes, and yoga studios.

Wellness Consultant

Advise individuals and organisations on holistic wellness, diet, lifestyle, and stress management strategies.

Researcher / Academic

Pursue postgraduate research in naturopathy or teach at naturopathic colleges contributing to the advancement of holistic medicine.

FAQs

Yes, BNYS has exceptional future prospects. With growing global interest in holistic and alternative medicine, naturopathic physicians are increasingly sought after in India and internationally. The wellness industry is one of the fastest growing sectors, creating diverse and rewarding opportunities for BNYS graduates.

BNYS graduates typically start with ₹20,000 to ₹40,000 per month in clinical settings. Those who establish their own naturopathic practice or wellness centre can earn significantly more. International opportunities in countries with recognized naturopathy systems offer substantially higher compensation packages.

Candidates must have completed Class 12 with PCB subjects scoring minimum 50% marks. Submit the online application with Class 12 marksheets and required documents. After verification, selected candidates complete admission and fee formalities. Visit our Admissions page or contact our office for detailed guidance.

The Ministry of AYUSH (Ayurveda, Yoga, Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy) actively promotes and regulates naturopathy education and practice in India. Government AYUSH hospitals and wellness centres regularly recruit BNYS graduates. The National Naturopathy Hospital in Pune and various state AYUSH departments offer government employment opportunities.
